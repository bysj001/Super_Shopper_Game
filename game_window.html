Super Shopper

In My Cart

How to Play:

use your arrow keys to navigate your shopper around the store. To view a shelf, direct the shopper toward the shelf of your choosing. Collide your shopper with the shelf to 'inspect' the shelf's contents. From here, you may use your mouse pad to select a food item on the shelf, view it's nutrition label, add it to your cart, or go back to the main game window. Enjoy and remember, always shop responsibly!
